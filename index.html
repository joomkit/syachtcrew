Yacht Crew Help Fundraiser

Working on a superyacht can be a dream come true. 

What's the problem?

Stress

67% of crew experience high levels of work-related stress

Isolation

48% suffer from social isolation or loneliness

Mental health

  • 37% want access to mental health services

ISWAN's Solution

Our solution is to use our unique expertise to build a toolkit that includes direct line to free, 24-hour emotional support, guidance, and useful health and welfare resources.
Through this project we will provide:

01.

Online 24/7/365 chat support

02.

Specially developed mental health resources

03.

Access to work related resolution pathways

04.

Financial planning and dispute assistance
